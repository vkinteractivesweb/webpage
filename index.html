Welcome to VK INTERACTIVES

We are an independent software development and creative studio dedicated to crafting immersive experiences and innovative applications. From story-driven games to utility software, our mission is to deliver quality and fun to our users. Explore our projects, meet the team, and join our community!

ABOUT US

​VK INTERACTIVES is an independent studio driven by a shared passion for crafting memorable interactive experiences. We are a small team of creators, pouring our heart and soul into building unique worlds, like our ambitious narrative game, To the Life. While our focus is on indie game development, we also leverage our creative skills to produce quality utility software, such as Mood Tracker and Motivateme, to enhance daily life. Every project we launch is a labor of love, representing our commitment to originality and connecting with players on a personal level.

OUR AIM

​Our aim at VK INTERACTIVES is to be an independent development studio that creates deeply engaging and original digital experiences. We are driven by the passion to craft immersive worlds for players in the indie game space, while also applying our technical skills to develop practical, high-quality utility software. Leveraging the collective creativity and dedication of our small team, our goal is to consistently deliver polished, thoughtful, and memorable products across all our categories.
